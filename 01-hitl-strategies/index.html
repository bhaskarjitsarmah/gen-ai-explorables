Keeping humans in the loop of LLM agents

A map of 23 human-in-the-loop strategies for LLM agents — from quick preference clicks to full approval gates and drift-triggered oversight. Filter by family, walk the agent lifecycle, and see the evidence behind each one.

Two questions, every strategy

Each one answers which kind of human involvement, and when in the agent's life it happens.

The "when" axis → columns

Strategies apply at design time, runtime, or post-deployment. Many span more than one.

The "how much" axis → spectrum

Involvement ranges from approving every step to merely watching for drift. That's the strip below.
